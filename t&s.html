TECHNIQUES AND STRATEGIES

Forehand Drive

The forehand drive is one of the most fundamental and effective shots in pickleball. It is executed with a forward, controlled swing on the player's dominant side, generating speed, accuracy, and consistency. Commonly used during baseline rallies and transition play, the forehand drive allows players to apply pressure, create offensive opportunities, and maintain control of the point. Mastering this shot is essential for players of all skill levels.
